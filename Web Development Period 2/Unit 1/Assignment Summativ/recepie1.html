My modern take on Italian-Indian fusion: Palak Chicken Penne Pasta

A rich, modern Italian–Indian fusion that marries the creaminess of penne pasta with the vibrant earthiness of North Indian palak chicken curry. Perfect for when you want comforting pasta with a twist of bold spices.

This recipe is designed to showcase how two culinary traditions can work together seamlessly: the comforting flavors of spinach-based chicken curry are paired with the familiar texture of penne pasta. It’s hearty, flavorful, and perfect for both weeknight dinners and special occasions.

The dish brings not only taste but also vibrant color to your table, making it as visually appealing as it is satisfying to eat.

For the Chicken & Marinade

  • 300 g boneless chicken breast/thigh, diced into bite-sized cubes
  • 1 tbsp yogurt
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp lemon juice

For the Palak Sauce

  • 250 g spinach (palak), washed & blanched
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2–3 green chilies (adjust to spice preference)
  • 2 tbsp oil (or ghee)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp cumin powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • ½ tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 100 ml fresh cream (or milk for a lighter option)
  • Salt to taste

For the Pasta + Garnish

  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  • Grated parmesan (optional, to bridge the Italian side)
  • 300 g penne pasta
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Pinch of salt

How To Prepare:

  1. Prep the Chicken
    1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt.
    2. Marinate the chicken pieces for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the Pasta
    1. Boil penne pasta in salted water until al dente.
    2. Drain, drizzle with olive oil, and set aside.
  3. Make the Palak Base
    1. Blanch spinach leaves in hot water for 2–3 minutes, then shock in cold water.
    2. Blend spinach and green chilies into a smooth puree.
  4. Cook the Curry
    1. Heat oil in a deep pan. Add cumin seeds and bay leaf.
    2. Sauté onions until golden brown. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
    3. Stir in tomato puree, turmeric, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
    4. Add the spinach puree, garam masala, and kasuri methi. Simmer gently.
    5. Stir in cream for richness.
  5. Cook the Chicken
    1. In a separate pan, sear marinated chicken until golden and cooked through.
    2. Add the chicken into the palak curry. Mix well.
  6. Fusion Finish
    1. Toss the cooked penne pasta into the palak chicken curry.
    2. Mix gently to coat pasta without breaking it.
    3. Adjust seasoning and creaminess to preference.
  7. Garnish & Serve
    1. Top with chopped coriander and (optionally) parmesan shavings.
    2. Serve hot with garlic bread or enjoy it as a standalone meal.
